About

Welcome to the Soft Skills Puzzle! This puzzle was designed to challenge your problem-solving and critical thinking skills, while also providing a fun and engaging experience.

The puzzle consists of 5 clues that are designed to be solved in sequence. Each clue will test a different set of skills, and as you progress through the puzzle, the clues will become progressively more difficult.

To solve the puzzle, you'll need to use a combination of logic, deduction, and creativity. Don't worry if you get stuck - each clue provides hints to help you along the way. And if you get really stuck, don't hesitate to play again!

Good luck, and have fun!
